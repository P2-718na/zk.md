#####  `DEF` Metrica
$d: \mathbb{X}\times\mathbb{X} \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^+ :=$ `metrica` $:\Leftrightarrow$
    1. $d(x, y) = 0 \iff x = y$
    2. $d(x, y) = d(y, x)$
    3. $d(x, y) < d(x, z) + d(z, y)$
